Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 9, 2026

🛡️ Absolute Privacy Commitment: Attention Replay is designed with a local-first architecture. We do not run any remote tracking servers, we do not collect your personal data, and we do not transmit any of your browsing history to the cloud. All tracking data remains 100% on your local machine.

1. Information We Access and Track

Attention Replay accesses your active browsing tabs and duration metrics locally in order to perform tracking calculations. This data includes:

  • Web History & Domains: The domain names of websites you actively visit.
  • Time Spent: The duration of your active focus or open status on each domain.
  • Idle States: Inactivity states (such as away-from-keyboard or device locked) to pause tracking and maintain accurate analytics.

2. Data Storage and Retention

All data processed by Attention Replay is stored locally using the Chrome Extension Local Storage API (chrome.storage.local). This data is kept privately inside your browser profile. The extension does not utilize external cloud backups, nor does it upload your stats to any server. If you clear the extension data or uninstall the extension, all collected records are permanently deleted from your device.

3. Third-Party Sharing

We do not share, sell, lease, or transfer your browsing activity, history, or metrics to any third parties. Since we do not transmit your data off your local device, it is impossible for us to access or share it.

4. Remote Code Disclosures

Attention Replay complies with extension store policies and contains no remote code or dynamic script execution. All resources, libraries, and logic are fully packaged locally within the extension archive.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in extension features or store policy requirements. Any updates will be pushed directly with new version updates to the store.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or feedback about our local privacy design, feel free to contact us at: haiderplayhub@gmail.com.
